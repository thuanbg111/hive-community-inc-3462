About Hive Community, Inc.

Hive Community, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) public charity (EIN 994093462) based in the United States. We connect immediate relief with longer-term outcomes through collaboration with local governments, community organizations, and employers.

Community-first

We design programs around real-world barriers: transportation, documents, and trust. Services are located where people actually show up.
